    How to read this dashboard
    • Overall risk condenses all 12 Tox21 assays into a single score from 0–1, shown on the gauge as Low, Medium, or High toxicity risk.
    • Assay scores show which specific nuclear receptor (NR) or stress response (SR) pathways light up most strongly for this molecule.
    • Features are structural fragments (Morgan fingerprint bits) that most strongly push the model toward toxicity for this compound.
    About Tox21
    The Tox21 program is a collaboration between U.S. agencies to screen thousands of chemicals against a collection of toxicity assays, including nuclear receptor and stress response pathways. This demo uses a compact model trained on a subset of public Tox21 data to estimate toxicity risk directly from SMILES strings.
